EMPOWERING FUTURES THROUGH OPPORTUNITIES

Your gateway to valuable internships and opportunities for first-generation, low-income, international, and undocumented students. Explore Internship/Fellowship opportunities all year round!

MISSION

STATEMENT

We're learners, teachers, creatives, designers, psychologists, engineers, and more.

We believe that personal growth drives true self-actualization. Our mission is to empower every student to find the right opportunities and reach their fullest potential.

Reflecting the diversity and breadth of our team, we offer tools for you to:

Find the perfect opportunity for you

Belong and feel part of a community

Grow your skills and shape your career

What We

Offer

1

Curated Opportunities Database

We provide a comprehensive and regularly updated database of internship and fellowship opportunities tailored specifically for first-generation, low-income, international, and undocumented students

2

Opportunities Insights

Access detailed information on each program, including deadlines, requirements, and categories, to make informed decisions and stay organized throughout your application process.

3

Streamlined Application Process

Easily find and apply for opportunities with our intuitive search, bookmarking, and location-based filtering features.

4

Community Empowerment

We enhance accessibility to relevant opportunities and provide contact information for previous students willing to assist with questions and application processes.
